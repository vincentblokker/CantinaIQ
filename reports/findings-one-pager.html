§ 05 Limitations the committee must hold
  1. Selection bias. Producers without a Vivino footprint are invisible to the pipeline; small natural-wine producers are systematically under-counted.
  2. Vintage compression. Ratings are mean-pooled across in-scope vintages, which under-weights producers with strong recent runs.
  3. Single-platform anchoring. Review distributions are drawn from one consumer platform — palate bias is Anglophone.
